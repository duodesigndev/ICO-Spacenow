    About

    Spacenow are Pioneers of the Activated Space Economy

    We activate a near limitless range of underutilised spaces on the most trusted platform to drive growth and opportunity. Spacenow unlike any other collaborative space or service provider is a truly mega platform. We redefine space, any space and make it available for others. More than sharing, we provide access to monetise that which you don’t use and provide the exchange to acquire that which you don’t. Spacenow is the world’s first space activation economy because we are activating all space for the benefit of individuals. Welcome to the new era of Activated-Spaces.

    The Problem

    We don’t have a real estate problem, we have a technology problem.

    In 2016, there were more than 11,000 co-working spaces available worldwide. This is predicted to grow at a rate of 23.8%. But the number of co-working members is set to explode even more rapidly, at a rate of 41% compounded annually. At the same time, commercial spaces around the world sit empty. During the rst quarter of 2017, the global of ce vacancy rate was measured at 11.9% and climbing.

    Not to mention empty desks, function spaces, photo studios, hotel lobbies, and more that are underutilized and wasting precious resources.

    There’s more than enough workspace to meetthe growing demands of remote workers, but there is no single, trustworthy platform thatbrings together remote workers and those with space all over the world.

    The Solution

    The solution is Spacenow, a world- 1st decentralized platform for short-term leasing that activates any space in the world which is underutilized.

    It’s a world- rst platform that instantly connects and empowers our two target audiences worldwide: hosts with spaces to lease or sublease; and guests or businesses looking for exible working space. All hosts are able to monetize and manage their own space. And guests have control over how long they want to stay and the type of space they’re looking for.

    The open-ended opportunities facilitated by the platform drive innovation and new opportunities and revenue streams globally.If someone has a space, no matter how small or large, they will be able to activate it.

    Blockchain, ICO & Cryptocurrencies

    Since Bitcoin’s launch in 2009, many additional blockchain protocols have surfaced – each facilitating blockchain adoption within certain industries. For example, Ethereum, Ripple, Hyperledger, Corda, Neo, etc. Although Bitcoin is the dominate blockchain in terms of popularity and valuation, its smart contract protocol is most suitable for simple value exchange agreements. Ethereum is currently the most widely used blockchain protocol for platforms whose business logic requires a more sophisticated smart contract implementation.
